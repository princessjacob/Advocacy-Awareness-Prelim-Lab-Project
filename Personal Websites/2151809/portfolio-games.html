THE PRISONER

Plot

A young princess is sent to prison without any idea of her wrongdoing. They said that it's murder. But the princess denies this outright.

A prisoner, the princess' jailmate is someone who had been in prison twice. A man who spouts pure sarcasm and acts like some sort of hero. But behind him is a shadow of someone who's once an innocent child.

A young warden, someone who bears a pure talent of manipulation but disliked it in many ways. A sarcastic young man who stood on steel beliefs. He likes to think that he's saving people but is he really?

Other Information

  • Platform: Windows
  • Engine used: Ren'Py
  • Genre: Role Playing, Visual Novel
  • Estimated playtime: 2 hours


DIE.ALONE?

Plot

M-C lives her life in a manor with a reknowned doctor and her dependable yet jittery nurse. The medicine works fine. It helps her stop seeing the ghastly shadows that had always been a huge part of her life.
But a simple miss out on taking her medicine flushes everything down the drain until she can't control it anymore. That being said, is it alright to take the medicine in the first place?

Other Information

  • Platform: Windows
  • Genre: Role Playing, Exploration, Puzzle, Visual Novel
  • Estimated playtime: 2-3 hours

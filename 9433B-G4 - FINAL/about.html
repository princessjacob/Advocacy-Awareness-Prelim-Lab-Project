About Us

This site is developed by BS Information Technology students who wants to promote and share what Inclusive Education is to everyone.


Why are we doing this?

We want to share the benefits of having an Inclusive Education to people who don't have knowledge about it. We also want to see all children learning together and giving the special ones opportunity to learn like a normal child.
